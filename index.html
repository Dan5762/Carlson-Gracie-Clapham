Welcome to Carlson Gracie Clapham

Welcome to our martial arts academy, where our head instructor, Martin Foot, brings 39 years of martial arts experience to the forefront.

A Supportive Environment

To Challenge Yourself

Frequently Asked Questions

For your trial class at Carlson Gracie Clapham, you will be paired with an experienced member who'll guide you through the basics. You should wear sports clothes (shorts/tracksuit and sports top) and our classes run 60-90 minutes and include warm-ups, technique instruction, and optional sparring at your comfort level.

While there's a lot to learn initially, you'll quickly discover the fun side of jiu jitsu. Our experienced training partners and coaches ensure you feel supported from day one.

Carlson Gracie Clapham offers monthly memberships starting at £50 for one class per week, £70 for two classes per week, or £90 for unlimited classes. Your first trial class is completely free. Gis (uniforms) are purchased separately. All memberships are monthly commitments. All classes are included in your membership with no hidden fees or premium class charges. Start with your free trial and find the membership that fits your schedule at South London's friendliest jiu jitsu gym.

Yes! Jiu jitsu at Carlson Gracie Clapham is excellent for both fitness and weight loss. Most members lose weight while building core strength and improving cardiovascular fitness. The intensity is entirely up to you and your training partner - from slow and technical to high heart rate workouts. You'll often be so focused during sparring that you won't notice how hard you're working, making it more engaging than traditional gym workouts. You'll typically see fitness improvements within a few months. While some members add strength training and stretching, jiu jitsu alone provides a complete workout.
