Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my first class?

For your first class, just bring comfortable workout clothes (athletic shorts and a t-shirt), water, and an open mind. We'll provide everything else you need to get started. Gym equipment and mats are available at the academy.

Do I need any prior experience?

No prior experience is necessary! Our classes are designed to accommodate complete beginners. Our experienced instructors will guide you through the fundamentals and help you progress at your own pace.

What is Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu?

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is a martial art and combat sport that focuses on ground fighting and submission techniques. It emphasizes technique and leverage over strength, making it suitable for people of all sizes and fitness levels.

How often should I train?

For beginners, we recommend starting with 2-3 classes per week. This allows your body to adapt while building a strong foundation. As you progress, you can increase your training frequency based on your goals and schedule.

Is BJJ safe?

Safety is our top priority. BJJ is generally very safe when practiced properly under qualified instruction. We emphasize controlled training, proper technique, and respect for training partners to minimize the risk of injury.
