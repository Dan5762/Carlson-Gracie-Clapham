Absolutely, women are not only welcome but are a vital and growing part of the global BJJ community! Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is uniquely suited for women because it emphasises technique, leverage, and strategy over brute strength or size. This means it's an incredibly effective self-defense system that empowers a smaller individual to control and defeat a larger, stronger opponent.

Carlson Gracie Clapham actively fosters a supportive and inclusive environment for women, to build confidence and competence to compete.

BJJ provides immense benefits and we encourage all women to experience the transformative power of BJJ!
