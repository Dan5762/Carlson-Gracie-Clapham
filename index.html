Why Train at Our South London BJJ Gym?

Carlson Gracie Clapham has become the destination BJJ gym for practitioners across London. Our students regularly travel from all corners of the city—and some even from outside London—to train with us. Here's why our gym stands out:

Prime South London Location

Perfectly positioned on the borders of Lambeth and Wandsworth, our BJJ gym in London is ideally located for residents across South London. Whether you're in Clapham, Battersea, Brixton, Stockwell, Vauxhall, or Nine Elms, you'll find us easily accessible and convenient for your training schedule.

Excellent Transport Links

Our gym is incredibly well-connected, making it one of the most accessible BJJ gyms in London:

  • 10-minute walk from Clapham Common tube station (Northern Line)
  • Direct bus routes: 77, 87, and 452 from Vauxhall, Clapham Junction, and surrounding areas

  • Easy access from Battersea, Stockwell, and Kennington stations
